Grand Coding Competition

Event

06/03/2022 | By LMVM2041

As everyone is slowly finishing the school year and beginning the summer season, Roblox Studio Central wants to introduce the Grand Coding Competition! This competition will take place entirely in Roblox Studio and will use LUA programming code to complete various tasks. After each task, the community will vote for the best creations, and those people move on to the next round!

During these next few days, we invite all developers of all skill levels to sign up and try out!

Prizes

  • 1st Place: Discord Nitro Classic
  • 2nd Place: VIP Role in the Discord Server
  • 3rd Place: Custom role in the Discord Server

The competition is simple! There will be 3 rounds which will consist of different tasks the participants much complete. Once the round is complete, voting will take place and the participants with the most votes proceed to the next round!

After the third round, the winner will be determined and awarded!
