Official Discord Rules

20/04/2022 | By LMVM2041

Please read the rules carefully. Moderators and all ranks higher have final say when it comes to moderation in Roblox Studio Central.

1. Chat Rules

1.A. Be respectful towards other members. You should not swear or disrespect at or towards other members in any way, including voice chats.

1.B. Swearing is moderately allowed. Please follow rules 1.A when it comes to this rule.

1.C. No spamming is allowed. This includes word walls, spam pings, emojis, words and gifs.

1.D. Links are not allowed (ignore if you're a VIP). Dyno will automatically delete links.

1.E. NSFW is strictly prohibited. This includes pornography, roleplay, links, and discussions related to NSFW. This is a bannable offence.

1.F. Innapropriate conversations include racism, sexism, controversial and sensitive topics, drugs/alcohol. These types of conversations are prohibited.

1.G. Follow Discord TOS. Harassment, discrimination, and impersonations are prohibited and you will be reported.

1.H. Advertising is not allowed in any way (Voice chats included).

2. Voice Chat Rules

2.A. Earrape is prohibited.

2.B. Follow all rules stated in section 1.

3. Marketplace

3.A. Scamming is prohibited and is a serious offence. Scams are investigated by our investigations team and punishments will be given for any perpetrators involved in a scam in this server.

4. General Rules

4.A. Keep nicknames short and appropriate.

4.B. Profile pictures must abide by rule 1.E.
