Verified Developer Program Application

Requirements

  • The developer must be signing up individually. A group of developers cannot sign up.
  • The developer must have obtained and completed at least 3 success commissions.
  • The developer must be 13 years of age or older.
  • The developer must have at least 1 year of development experience.
  • The developer must have valid photographic proof of one or more of their project(s) posted.
